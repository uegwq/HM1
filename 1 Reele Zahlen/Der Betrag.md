## Definition
***
$|a|= \begin{cases} a, & \text{falls } a \geq 0 \\ -a, & \text{falls } a < 0 \end{cases}$
## Regeln
***
1. $|-a| = |a|$ und $|a-b| = |b-a|$
2. $|a| \ge 0$
3. $|a| = 0 \iff a = 0$
4. $|ab|=|a||b|$
5. $a \le |a|$ und $-a\le |a|$
6. $|a+b|\le |a|+|b|$
7. $||a|-|b||\le|a-b|$
